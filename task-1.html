Feed the Future

Providing nutritious meals to orphaned children in need

About Us

Feed the Future is a non-profit initiative dedicated to ending child hunger among orphans. Our mission is to provide regular, healthy meals and create sustainable support for orphanages across the country.

Our Services

We focus on practical, impactful programs that serve the nutritional and educational needs of orphans.

Daily Meal Program

Nutritious breakfast, lunch, and dinner provided to registered orphanages.

Frequency: Daily

Food Donation Drives

Collecting packaged and fresh food from individuals and local businesses.

Frequency: Weekly

Nutrition Education

Workshops teaching children and caregivers about healthy eating habits.

Frequency: Monthly

Partnership with Farms

Collaboration with local farms to source fresh fruits and vegetables.

Frequency: Ongoing
